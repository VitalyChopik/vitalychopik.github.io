Meet Luke

Luke Timmins serves as Managing Director for Shire Oak #Indonesia, a developer and financier of distributed and large-scale #solar and #renewableenergy projects.

He also serves as a Senior Advisor to TES on their second life #ESS and battery repurposing business.

Prior to this he was CEO for a Vietnam focused solar platform, owned by investment management firm VinaCapital (VOF.L) and Vice President in the Infrastructure & Energy division at Australian investment bank,Macquarie Capital (MQG.ASX).

Luke is a strong proponent of the notion that solar technologies have a crucial function in addressing several prevailing global challenges beyond the obvious climate change mitigation (SDG13) and energy-access issues (SGD7).

He believes that more distributed solar infrastructure will result in the unlocking of expansive opportunities across societies in areas of health (SDG3), education (SDG4), industry, innovation infrastructure (SDG9), and general equality (SDG10).

His career started in China where he built and exited 2 renewable energy start-ups. He has a history of building high-performance teams, driving market entry and operational excellence in the renewable energy industry, even in periods of economic contraction.

Experience

Across his 17-year career in renewable energy,
he held management and operational positions at

  • Kyocera Solar (6971.TYO)
  • Hanergy Thin Film Power (0566.HK)
  • Emergya Wind Technologies
  • Phoenix Solar (PS4.F)

In addition to his focus on utility-scale solar, energy storage, and microgrids, he has a penchant for rooftop solar, which he views as the most captivating growth segment; he has worked on a multitude of rooftop solar projects for MNCs such as IKEA, Bosch, and Toyota, as well as for regional conglomerates committed to #RE100 goals across Asia Pacific.

He has completed Executive General Management Programmes at INSEAD and University of Cambridge (both alumni status programmes).

He has completed technical certifications in solar & wind system design, #energystorage, #O&M and HSE equipping him with detailed knowledge of development, execution and operational stages of IPP projects.

Luke has been situated across Asia Pacific for most of his career. He regards the Asia region as the solar industry’s current centre of gravity and a strategic base for future deployment of the forthcoming gigawatts globally.

In Addition to Asia, Luke actively works on developing renewable energy in Sub-Saharan Africa as well as island nations.

Luke has a track record of delivering concrete results, understands building outcome-driven relationships in Asia and manages front-line teams worldwide.

He is a combination of commercial strategist, technically adept and an operational leader. He is a native English speaker (British and New Zealand nationalities) and proficient in Mandarin, gained during 7 years of living in China.

Interests include sailing, golf and long-distance running. British-born, he grew up across Bermuda, New Zealand, and Australia. He is passionate about the environment and being a good custodian on the earth.
